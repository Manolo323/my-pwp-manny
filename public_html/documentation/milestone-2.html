Content Strategy

My site will be one page long and will be structured in the following sections:

Welcome

A welcome message with a brief introduction of my self and what inspired me to create this website.

The Basics

This section will cover the fundamental knowledge needed to understand the basics of setting up their guitars. Thi will include definitions, key terms, and tuning of each string with acronyms to help remember notes.

Step-By-Step Tutorials

This section will have the main content. It will provide detailed instructions on how to position their fingers and thename of each chord. I will also include diagrams and images of my fingers.

Resources

There will be additional resources attached to this section for more advanced players.
