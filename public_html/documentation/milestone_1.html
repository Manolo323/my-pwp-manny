Mile-Stone 1

Purpose, Audience and Goal

Purpose: This project will be a tutorial website for beginner guitar players looking to expand their skills

Audience: The audience of this website is likely to be individuals who are interested in learning to play the guitar but have little or no previous experience with the instrument.

Goal: The goal of this website is to provide beginner-level tutorials for people that may not have the time or recourses to take formal guitar lessons and are interested in learning at their own pace.

Persona

Name: Sam Green

Age: 25

Occupation: College Student

Tech: Sam is comfortable using her MacBook Air for her studies and an iPhone 13

Attitudes and Behaviours: Sam is a college student with a passion for music. She has always been interested in learning to play the guitar, but has never had the opportunity to take lessons. She recently bought her first guitar and is now looking for a website with tutorials for beginner guitar players. She has no knowledge of music theory and wants to learn how to play chords, strumming patterns, and simple songs.

Frustrations and Needs: Sam finds it frustrating to find the right website for her guitar tutorials. She is not sure which website is reliable and trust worthy, and is worried that she might waste her time on a website that does not offer the quality of instructions she needs. She also needs a website that is affordable and fits her budget.

Goal: This website can help Sam by providing her with a comprehensive set of beginner-level guitar tutorials for free. The website can offer clean and concise instructions, with diagrams and videos to help her understand the material. By providing Sam with the resources she needs to learn guitar, this website can help her achieve her goal of becoming a skilled guitar player.

User Story: As a user, I want find a website that provides quality guitar tutorials that are easy to understand, easy to follow, and will let me learn at my own pace.
